# Bill C-239: An Act to amend the Canada Health Act (accountability)

## Summary

This enactment amends the `Canada Health Act` to require provinces to implement an accountability framework in relation to the delivery of health services in order to qualify for a full cash contribution as part of the Canada Health Transfer.

His Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and House of Commons of Canada, enacts as follows:


## Canada Health Act

### Section 4

The purpose of this Act is to establish criteria, conditions and **requirements** in respect of insured health services and extended health care services provided under provincial law that must be met before a full cash contribution may be made.

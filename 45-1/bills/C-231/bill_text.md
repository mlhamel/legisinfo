# Bill C-231: An Act to amend the Youth Criminal Justice Act

## Summary

This enactment amends the `Youth Criminal Justice Act` in order to

clarify the measures governing addiction treatment programs for young persons;

enable, in some cases, the youth justice court that finds a young person guilty of an offence to delay sentencing to enable the young person to participate in an addiction treatment program;

enable the youth justice court to include in certain orders the condition of attending an addiction treatment program; and

provide that failing or refusing to comply with such a condition cannot by itself result in a custodial sentence.

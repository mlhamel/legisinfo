# Bill C-220: An Act to amend the Criminal Code (immigration status in sentencing)

## Summary

This enactment amends the `Criminal Code` to provide that, in imposing a sentence on an offender who is not a Canadian citizen, a court must not take into consideration the offenderâs immigration status in Canada.

His Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and House of Commons of Canada, enacts as follows:


## Criminal Code

### Section 1

The `Criminal Code` is amended by adding the following after section 718.201:

### Section 718.202

A court that imposes a sentence on an offender who is not a Canadian citizen shall not take into consideration any potential impact the sentence could have on the offenderâs immigration status in Canada, or on that of a member of their family.

# Bill S-222: An Act to amend the Canada Elections Act and the Regulation Adapting the Canada Elections Act for the Purposes of a Referendum 

**Sponsor**: Senator M

## Summary

This enactment amends the `Canada Elections Act` and the `Regulation Adapting the Canada Elections Act for the Purposes of a Referendum` to extend the right to vote to citizens aged 16 and 17.

His Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and House of Commons of Canada, enacts as follows:


## Short Title

### Section 1

This Act may be cited as the *Vote 16 Act*.

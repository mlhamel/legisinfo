# Bill S-207: An Act to amend the Criminal Records Act, to make consequential amendments to other Acts and to repeal a regulation

**Sponsor**: Senator PATE

## Summary

This enactment amends the `Criminal Records Act` to provide for the expiry of criminal records. It also makes consequential amendments to other Acts and repeals a regulation.

## Effect of a Record **Expiry**

### Section 2.2

A record expiry

is evidence of the fact that the conviction for an offence in respect of which a record expiry has occurred should no longer reflect adversely on the character of the person who was convicted of the offence; and

unless it is subsequently revoked or ceases to have effect, requires that

Whereas diffuse intrinsic pontine glioma is an aggressive brain tumour that attacks the brainstem and slowly destroys all vital functions even as cognitive functions remain intact, rendering the affected person a prisoner in their own body;

Whereas diffuse intrinsic pontine glioma typically affects children who are five to seven years of age;

Whereas diffuse intrinsic pontine glioma remains inoperable, incurable and fatal;

Whereas the prognosis and treatments have not improved in more than 40 years;

Whereas the Parliament of Canada wishes to increase awareness of this disease among Canadians in order to encourage public and private investment in research to improve the prognosis and treatments;

Whereas it is important to honour those who have died of this disease;

And whereas the 17th day of May of each year is recognized in a number of other countries as a diffuse intrinsic pontine glioma awareness day;

His Majesty, by and with the advice and consent of the Senate and House of Commons of Canada, enacts as follows:

### Section 1

This Act may be cited as the *National Diffuse Intrinsic Pontine Glioma Awareness Day Act*.
